Intelligent Systems for Engineering.

Aleph Sim is a specialized consultancy focused on developing AI and mathematical solutions for engineering and scientific research. We tackle complex problems at the intersection of machine learning, numerical simulation, and domain-specific engineering challenges.

Our approach combines cutting-edge AI techniques with rigorous mathematical methods to create intelligent systems that analyze, optimize, and enhance engineering workflows.

101 Founder

About the owner:

Fig. 1 Founder · 2026
Hrvoje Molnar

I graduated from the University of Zagreb with a degree in Electrical Engineering, then spent a year at KU Leuven as an Erasmus student studying Data Science. Two paths that I couldn't quite pick between — fortunately, I didn't have to.

The fork resolved itself during an internship at Infineon Technologies AG, where I got to develop machine-learning approaches inside semiconductor R&D. From that point on I was set: engineering background, data-science toolkit, and a real appetite for problems that need both.

I don't think of myself as a pure data scientist or a pure engineer. I'm a bit of both — and I enjoy the projects that require the combination.

Let's build something that runs.
