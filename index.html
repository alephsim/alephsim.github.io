10 — ... and little about me :)

The founder's story

I graduated from the University of Zagreb with a degree in Electrical Engineering (BSc), learning from the best in the field. After that, I decided to pursue a degree in Data Science as an Erasmus student at KU Leuven, Belgium. Fascinated by both paths, I couldn't decide between them. But, fortunately, I didn't have to...

A great "deus-ex-machina" moment occured when I applied and got accepted for an internship position at Infineon Technologies AG. I had an opportunity to develop machine learning approaches in the field of semiconductors. At that point on, I was set on a path that combined my engineering background with data science and AI.

I do not think of myself as a pure data scientist, nor as a pure engineer. I am a bit of both, and I enjoy working on projects that require a combination of skills.
